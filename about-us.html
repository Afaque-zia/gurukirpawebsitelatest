About Us

Your Trusted Property Partner

Guru Kripa Real Estate is a well-established and trusted real estate consultancy based in Patna, offering comprehensive property solutions across key markets including Delhi, Noida, Gurugram, Patna, and Jharkhand. With a strong commitment to integrity, transparency, and client satisfaction, we have positioned ourselves as a dependable name in the real estate industry.

We specialize in residential properties, commercial properties, industrial properties, investment properties, open plots, land parcels, and upcoming developments. Whether clients are end-users seeking their dream home or investors pursuing high-return opportunities, we provide tailored solutions that align with their goals and budgets.

Guru Kripa Real Estate is owned and led by Mr. Kundan Mukherjee, a 13+ years experienced real estate professional with deep insight into regional and national property markets. His hands-on experience, market expertise, and ethical practices have helped the company build a strong reputation for reliability and long-term value creation.

At Guru Kripa Real Estate, we believe property decisions are milestones in our clients' lives. Our approach centers on understanding individual requirements, offering honest market advice, and ensuring smooth, transparent, and legally sound transactions. From property identification and site visits to negotiations and deal closure, we provide end-to-end support at every stage.

Our growing network, market research, and client-focused services enable us to deliver value-driven real estate solutions that meet modern market demands. We continuously strive to exceed expectations through professionalism, clear communication, and personalized service.
